**ANSWER TYPES:**

1. **Numerical**: Pure numbers (integers or decimals)
   - Examples: "5", "3.14", "130"

2. **Expression**: Mathematical expressions with symbols (π, √, fractions, variables)
   - Examples: "$\frac{1}{2}$", "$\sqrt{5}$", "$\frac{\pi}{4}$", "2H"
   - Keywords: "express in terms of", "symbolic form", "leave in exact form"

3. **Interval**: Ranges or intervals (a, b) or [a, b]
   - Examples: "(0, 10)", "[2, 5]", "($\frac{1}{3}H$, $2H$)"
   - Keywords: "range", "interval", "between", "from...to"

4. **Tuple**: Multiple separate values as coordinates or pairs
   - Examples: "(3, 1.57)", "(r, θ)"
   - Keywords: "in the form (a, b)", "coordinates", "pair"

5. **Equation**: Answer is an equation itself
   - Examples: "$x^2 + y^2 = 1$", "$F = ma$"
   - Keywords: "equation", "relationship", "formula"

6. **Mixed Types**: Multiple answers of different types
   - Examples: "3, $\sqrt{5}$" (Numerical,Expression)
   - Keywords: "and", "respectively", multiple sub-questions
